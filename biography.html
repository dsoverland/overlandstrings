Biography

I have been playing violin for 70 years.
I have played with The Seventh Army Symphony, The Boston Pops (under Arthur Fiedler), the St. Paul Chamber Orchestra, and the Golden Strings.

I started the string program for the Mounds View Schools in 1964. I was part of the strings program for 30 years and retired in 1993.  During that time, I also did private lessons during that time and since I have retired. I have taught as many as 30 students at one time. Some of my students have gone on to play for the St. Paul Chamber Orchestra and the Minnesota Orchestra.

I teach Violin and Viola.
